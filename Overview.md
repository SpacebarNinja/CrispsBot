## Species Traits

**Tiefling / Glasya** — Viola
- Darkvision — See in dim light as bright, darkness as dim, up to 60 ft
- Hellish Resistance — Take half damage from fire
- Infernal Legacy (Glasya) — Know Minor Illusion cantrip; cast Disguise Self once/day at level 3; cast Invisibility once/day at level 5

---

**Dhampir** — Isaiah
- Darkvision — See in dim light as bright, darkness as dim, up to 60 ft
- Vampiric Bite — Fanged bite is an unarmed strike; on hit, heal HP equal to your proficiency bonus
- Spider Climb — As a bonus action, gain a climb speed equal to your walk speed (can climb ceilings) until end of turn

---

**Aarakocra** — Aeran
- Flight — Flying speed of 50 ft (can't fly in medium or heavy armor)
- Talons — Natural unarmed strike dealing 1d6 slashing damage

---

**Goblin** — Bablino
- Darkvision — See in dim light as bright, darkness as dim, up to 60 ft
- Fury of the Small — Once per rest, deal bonus damage equal to your level when hitting a larger creature
- Nimble Escape — Disengage or Hide as a bonus action each turn

---

**Wood Elf** — Fey
- Darkvision — See in dim light as bright, darkness as dim, up to 60 ft
- Keen Senses — Proficiency in Perception
- Fey Ancestry — Advantage on saves vs charm; immune to magical sleep
- Trance — Only needs 4 hours of rest instead of 8
- Fleet of Foot — Movement speed is 35 ft instead of 30
- Mask of the Wild — Can attempt to hide when only lightly obscured by nature (leaves, rain, etc.)

---

**Kalashtar** — Steria
- Dual Mind — Advantage on Wisdom saving throws
- Mental Discipline — Resistance to psychic damage (take half)
- Mind Link — Can telepathically speak to any creature within 60 ft that you can see
- Severed from Dreams — Can't be targeted by the Dream spell; advantage against effects requiring sleep
